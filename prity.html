About Us

Krishi Mitra is dedicated to providing farmers with timely access to vital information, including weather forecasts, crop advisories, and mandi prices.

We offer a wide range of support and tools for farmers across India. From crop advisory to government schemes, our services empower farmers with timely and accurate information for better productivity and growth.
