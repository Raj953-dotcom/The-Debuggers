To get expert crop advisory based on soil, climate, and crop stage for maximizing yields, here’s a suggested step-by-step approach you can implement or include in your "Krishi Mitra – Farmer Support Portal":

---

1. Soil-Based Advisory


Soil Type & Texture: Sandy, loamy, clay, etc. Soil pH: Recommend crops and fertilizers that suit acidic, neutral, or alkaline soils. Nutrient Levels: Advise based on NPK (Nitrogen, Phosphorus, Potassium) analysis. Actions: Add organic matter if low fertility. Lime acidic soil or gypsum for alkaline soil.

2. Climate-Based Advisory



Temperature & Rainfall Patterns: Recommend crops suitable for the specific climate zone. Weather Forecast Integration: Suggest irrigation before heatwaves. Warn about fungal diseases before heavy rains. Microclimate Suggestions: Use of shade nets, windbreaks, or mulching.

3. Crop Stage Advisory


Germination/Seedling Stage: Recommend proper spacing, seed treatment, and light irrigation. Vegetative Stage: Nutrient-rich fertilizer schedules (e.g., nitrogen-rich for leafy growth). Flowering & Fruiting Stage: Pest and disease prevention (especially for borers, blights). Potassium and phosphorus support. Maturity & Harvesting: Irrigation reduction, pest control. Post-harvest handling and storage tips.
